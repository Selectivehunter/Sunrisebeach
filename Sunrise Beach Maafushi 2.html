About Us

Nestled in the heart of Maafushi Island, Sunrise Beach Guesthouse offers a perfect retreat for travelers seeking comfort, relaxation, and authentic local experiences.

Established in 1990, our guesthouse has been welcoming guests from around the world, providing them with exceptional hospitality and creating memorable stays.

Our dedicated team is committed to ensuring that every aspect of your stay exceeds your expectations. From our thoughtfully designed rooms to our personalized services, we take pride in attending to the smallest details.
